== Description ==

Easily insert any of your preexisting Wufoo forms directly through WordPress with the "Wufoo Forms" button. No more messy code; no more back and forth between browser windows. Just beautiful Wufoo forms. Simply follow the instructions to connect your account, and embed away!

Log in to try it today.

Advanced insertion (hosted) coming soon with your generous support. ;)



== Installation ==

1. Upload the `wufoo-integration` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Use the Wufoo Forms button (near the kitchen sink icon) on any Post or Page edit/add new screen.
1. After clicking the button for the first time, follow the simple setup instructions to connect your Wufoo account.
